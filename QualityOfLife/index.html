Quality of Your Life

Are you ok? Do you have clearly in mind what are the important things for yourself? Can you devote enough time to them ?

Life is just one, and passes quickly, better not wasting time on unpleasant things. This test, unpretentiously, wants to be food for thoughts, a quick way to dwell on trivial thoughts, but at the same time crucial for living a rich life .

The test is completely anonymous and nothing will be saved or stored, you can not retrieve a test done previously. At the end of the test if you want you can save an image with the result, or share the result on your facebook profile.

Le tue giornate tipo

Last step, we see actually how your day is divided

In the first part, in gray are the activities essential to living: eating, sleeping, having a salary

Surely these activities are a big chunk of your time and can not be avoided. If, however, those are a pleasure, indicates the hours in the next section, for example if we use two hours to go to the office, but if in those two hours you read a book (and you like read), consider your time devoted to a passion ....

The hours in the gray section does not affect the calculation of the final result, because like it or not affect vital activities.

If you see that you spend most of the day with these activities, try to think if there is a way to make them important to you. For example, if eating is not interesting for you, you may arrange lunch with friends, the time you use to eating becomes so pleasant
